# Belts and Spindle 

This chapter covers routing the GT2 belts.

---

## 1. Parts Required

| Qty  | Item                    | Source  | Notes                          |
|------|-------------------------|---------|--------------------------------|
| 3pc  | ~500mm GT2 6mm Belt     | Buy     | Measure carefully before cutting |
| 12pc | M3x10                   | Buy     | Some may be spare from Ender3 |
| 6pc  | Printed Belt Clamp      | Printed | Holds belt ends securely      |

---

## 2. Belt Installation

1. Route GT2 belts along X and Y axes.  
2. Use printed belt clamps to secure belt ends.  
3. Ensure belts are **tight but not overly stretched**.  
4. Test X/Y motion manually to confirm smooth travel.
