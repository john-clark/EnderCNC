# Troubleshooting

Common issues and fixes during your Ender 3 PRO CNC build.

---

## 1. Frame Issues

- **Frame not square:** Adjust diagonal measurements.  
- **XY joints binding:** Loosen wheels slightly and re-align.  

---

## 2. Carriage & Z-axis Issues

- **Z carriage binds:** Check MGN12H rail alignment.  
- **Leadscrew doesn’t turn smoothly:** Verify coupler alignment and thread engagement.

---

## 3. Belts & Spindle

- **Belts slipping:** Retighten or replace clamps.  
- **Spindle not secure:** Adjust printed clamp or use alternative spacer.

---

## 4. Wiring & Electronics

- **Limit switch not triggered:** Check wiring and press-fit alignment.  
- **Motors not moving:** Verify connections and firmware configuration.

---

### Tips

!!! tip
    Test movement manually before applying power or running G-Code.

!!! warning
    Always disconnect PSU before making wiring adjustments.
